Our Colorful Journey

Transforming spaces with passion, quality, and vibrant hues since 1995

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995 in the heart of Karunagapally, Amritha Traders began as a small family-owned paint store with a simple mission: to bring vibrant, high-quality paints to our community. What started as a modest shop has blossomed into one of the region's most trusted names in paints and coatings.

Over the years, we've grown from selling basic paints to offering a comprehensive range of interior, exterior, decorative, and specialty coatings, while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

"Colors are the smiles of nature. At Amritha Traders, we believe every space deserves to smile."

Our Colorful Timeline

Milestones in our journey of color

1995 - Humble Beginnings

Founded as a small paint retail store in Karunagapally by Mr. Rajeev Kumar with just 50 paint varieties

2002 - First Expansion

Opened our second location in Kollam, introducing professional painting services

2010 - Eco-Friendly Line

Launched our first zero-VOC eco-friendly paints, leading the green revolution in Kerala

2018 - Digital Transformation

Introduced color visualizer tools and online consultations, blending tradition with technology

2023 - Present Day

Now serving customers across South India with 300+ colors and comprehensive painting solutions
