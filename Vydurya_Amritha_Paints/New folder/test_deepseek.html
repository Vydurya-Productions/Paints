FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our paints and services

As a general rule, one liter of paint covers about 10-12 square meters of smooth surface. For textured surfaces, coverage may be less. Measure your walls (height x width) to calculate total area, subtract windows/doors, then divide by coverage rate. Our website has a paint calculator tool to help with this, or you can consult with our in-store experts.

Matte finishes have no shine and are great for hiding imperfections but are less washable. Satin (or eggshell) has a soft sheen, offers good durability, and is easier to clean. Gloss finishes are shiny, very durable and washable, but show surface imperfections more. We recommend matte for ceilings, satin for most walls, and gloss for trim, doors, and high-traffic areas.

With our premium WeatherShield exterior paint, you can expect 10-15 years of durability in most climates. In harsh coastal or desert environments, repainting may be needed every 7-10 years. Proper surface preparation and application are crucial for maximum longevity. Our 15-year warranty guarantees against peeling, cracking, and excessive fading when applied according to our specifications.
